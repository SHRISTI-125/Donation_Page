Educate Girls


Today nearly 120 million girls are out of school.

Girls are dropping out their studies for several reasons:

  • Sociocultural Norms
  • Financial Constraints
  • Household Responsibilities
  • Gender Bias
  • Lack of Support


    • Our Aim is to Support those girls who face challenges for their studies


  • Mentorship Programs : A mentorship program where girls interact with successful female role models.
    These mentors can provide guidance, support, and inspiration, helping girls to envision and strive for a brighter future.

  • Scholarships : Scholarships that cover tuition fees and other educational expenses.

  • Parental Education and Engagement : A program that educate parents about the importance of girls' education.

  • Technology and Resources : Helping with educational materials, technology, and resources that can enhance learning opportunities for girls.
    This can include digital devices, internet access, and educational software.
